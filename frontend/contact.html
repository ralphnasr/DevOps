Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

Go to My Orders to see your order status timeline. You'll see real-time updates as your order moves through confirmed, processing, shipped, and delivered stages. You'll also receive email notifications at each step.

How do I download my invoice?

Invoices are automatically generated when you place an order. Go to My Orders and click "Download Invoice" on the order card. A PDF invoice will also be emailed to you.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders in "confirmed" status can be cancelled from the Orders page. Once an order moves to "processing" or later, cancellation is no longer available. Stock is automatically restored upon cancellation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express). All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption. We also support Google Pay for faster checkout.

Is my personal information secure?

Absolutely. Your data is protected by industry-standard security measures including:
• SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit
• Encrypted storage for all personal data at rest
• Firewall protection against common web attacks
• Secure authentication with password hashing
• HTTPS enforced on all pages

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy on all items. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact us using the form below to initiate a return and we'll email you a prepaid return label.

How does shipping work?

Free standard shipping on orders over $50. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available at checkout. You can manage shipping addresses in your Account settings.

How do I create an account?

Click Sign Up and fill in your name, email, and password. You can also sign in with your Google account for a faster experience. An account lets you track orders, save wishlists, and manage your preferences.
